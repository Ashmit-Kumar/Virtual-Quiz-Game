HOW ✌️ PLAY

Login/Signup

Start by logging in or signing up to access the game. Once logged in, click on the "Start Quiz" button to initiate the game.

Interaction

Use two fingers to interact with the game. Keep in mind that the joining of two fingers Index and Middle finger only on the camera screen will be counted as a click. This tactile interaction adds a unique and engaging element to the gameplay.

Mode Selection

ENDLESS

Play at your own pace, take your time to think through each question, and submit whenever you feel ready.

ARCADE

Challenge yourself to answer as many questions as possible within a thrilling 3-minute timeframe. Be prepared for fast-paced decision-making.

Answering Questions

Questions will appear on your camera screen.Carefully review the options and point to the correct answer with precision.Click to submit your answer and proceed to the next challenge.

Topic-Based Quiz

Topic-based quizzes are a unique learning tool designed to focus on a specific subject or topic, making the learning process more targeted and efficient. These quizzes are subject-specific, meaning they are designed to focus on a specific subject or topic, allowing players to learn or revise that particular topic in an engaging and interactive way. The quiz questions are tailored to the topic, ensuring that the content is relevant and beneficial to the player. Topic-based quizzes are not just about learning; they are also designed to be engaging and fun. They are designed to make learning enjoyable and exciting, which can motivate players to participate and improve their knowledge on the topic. This can be achieved by using various techniques such as interactive elements, engaging visuals, and challenging questions. .
